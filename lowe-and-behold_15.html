The makers

Four hands,
one studio.

We're Kerry and Ruby Lowe, a mother-daughter team on the Sunshine Coast. And yes, we are very pleased with our name.

Ruby studied ceramics at TAFE and brought the craft. Kerry came along for the clay and accidentally became part of the business.

Together we make windchimes, trinket dishes, wall hangings, sculptural pieces and the occasional thing that surprises even us.

Everything is made by hand in our home studio. We sell at local markets, take commissions and run beginner workshops for people who want to try something tactile, creative and a bit therapeutic.
